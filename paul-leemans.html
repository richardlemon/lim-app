Paul Leemans

At ASML, Paul is responsible for the IT collaboration and communication strategy. He is manager of the Collaboration Competence Center which creates new services and brings value-add business solutions on those platforms. He is also the manager of the Productivity Office, a group of consultants who help people use the collaboration tools more efficiently and effectively. Before joining ASML in 2011, Paul was with Philips where he introduced their first social collaboration platform. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and has 20 years of experience in the human computer interaction domain, of which the last 18 in the Microsoft domain.
